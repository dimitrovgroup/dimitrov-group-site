Patients scheduled for primary hip arthroplasty at USBALO "Prof. Boycho Boychev" complete the FUNCAP27 functional-capacity questionnaire pre-operatively and at defined post-operative intervals. The case series mirrors the knee-arthroplasty FUNCAP27 cohort, enabling cross-procedure comparisons of self-reported function after lower-limb joint replacement.
