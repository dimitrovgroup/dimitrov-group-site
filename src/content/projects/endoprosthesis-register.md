The Endoprosthesis Register is the group's foundational arthroplasty dataset. It records implant identifiers, perioperative variables, and follow-up outcomes for every primary and revision hip and knee arthroplasty performed at USBALO "Prof. Boycho Boychev", Sofia. The register acts as the parent cohort from which the group's prospective PHQ-9 and FUNCAP27 studies are drawn.
