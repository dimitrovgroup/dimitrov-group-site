Patients scheduled for primary knee arthroplasty at USBALO "Prof. Boycho Boychev" complete the nine-item Patient Health Questionnaire (PHQ-9) pre-operatively and at defined post-operative intervals. The study quantifies how depressive symptom severity evolves around the procedure and whether baseline mental health predicts functional recovery.
