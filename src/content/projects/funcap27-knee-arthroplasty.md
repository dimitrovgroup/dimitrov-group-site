Patients scheduled for primary knee arthroplasty at USBALO "Prof. Boycho Boychev" complete the FUNCAP27 functional-capacity questionnaire pre-operatively and at defined post-operative intervals. The case series characterises the trajectory of self-reported function from baseline through recovery and links it to the standard surgical outcome set.
